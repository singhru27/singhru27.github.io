About Me

Hi, I'm Rujul

I grew up in Cincinnati, Ohio - and I studied both Computer Science and Economics at Brown University.

My passion has always lied in finding innovative technical solutions to pressing problems. I strive to use my technical background to solve challenges, and have been working full time at Qatalyst partners (a technology focused investment bank). At Qatalyst I was a member of the deal team that executed the $12bn+ sale of Mailchimp to Intuit, and have worked on several industry defining M&A and strategic advisory assignments. I began my career in Fintech at Worldpay (now FIS). and spent several summers prior to graduation working in various software engineering roles at Dresner Partners, the Brown Science Center, and the Brown Critical Review.

Outside of school you can find me skiing (although good skiing can be hard to come by in the midwest), trying out restaurants, or playing with my two adorable dogs: Cookie and Lila.
